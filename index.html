Common challenges

The AI problems most SMBs are quietly dealing with

You know AI is important. But getting from "we should do something" to actual results is harder than the headlines suggest.

🤯

AI overwhelm — too many tools, too little clarity

New AI products launch daily. It's hard to know what's hype, what's relevant to your business, and where to even start.

🔗

Data sitting in silos, not doing any work

Your customer data, transaction history, and inventory numbers have real value — but they're scattered across systems that don't talk to each other.

⏳

Staff time lost to repetitive manual tasks

From chasing documents to copying data between spreadsheets — your team is doing work that AI could handle, while higher-value tasks wait.

💸

Uncertainty about ROI before committing

AI investments feel risky without a clear path to return. You need evidence that it'll work for a business your size before going all in.

🛠️

No in-house AI expertise to build or manage it

Hiring a full AI team isn't practical. But you still need someone who can design, implement, and hand off solutions your people can actually use.

🔒

Compliance and data privacy concerns

Especially in financial services, using AI responsibly means navigating regulations. That adds friction most vendors don't help you with.
